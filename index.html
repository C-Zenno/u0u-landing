Core Principles

Observer-Only

Baseline observes and represents. It does not intervene, recommend, or steer.

No Optimization

Adaptation without objectives. Scaffolding without targets. Structure without steering.

Behavior, Not Identity

Signals are admissible. Profiles are not. The person is never the model.

Categorical Refusals

No personalization, no nudging, no persuasion, no claims of improvement.
