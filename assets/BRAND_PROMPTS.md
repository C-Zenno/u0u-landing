# U0U BaseLine Brand Prompts

## Canonical Banner (with text)

Minimalist premium UI hero graphic on a dark background **#0e091c**.

A single, thin **glowing wave line** floats horizontally across the upper third of the canvas.
The wave is smooth, restrained, and elegant — no sharp peaks.
The glow **starts as indigo on the left**, stays cool and subtle, then **transitions to violet beginning at exactly 58% of the horizontal width**, becoming more luminous toward the center-right before gently fading out.

Wave color gradient:
* Left: deep indigo / electric blue
* Transition point: **58%**
* Right: soft violet / lavender glow

Glow is diffused, cinematic, and clean — no noise, no particles.

Below the wave, centered horizontally, four **glass-morphism UI capsules** contain the text:

**"We · adapt · to · you"**

Each word sits in its own rounded rectangle:
* Semi-transparent dark glass
* Soft inner blur
* Subtle outer glow
* Even spacing between capsules
* Small centered dot separators between words

Typography:
* Modern sans-serif
* Medium weight
* White text with very slight cool tint
* Calm, confident, premium

At the far right of the word **"you"**, a **tiny star-like glint** appears on the baseline — subtle, sharp, intentional (not decorative).

Lighting & mood:
* Deep, calm, premium
* High contrast but soft
* No gradients in the background beyond natural vignette
* No textures, no grain

Style references:
* futuristic
* adaptive systems
* calm intelligence
* premium wellness technology
* minimal, no clutter

Composition:
* Perfectly centered
* Balanced negative space
* Designed to feel **alive, adaptive, and inevitable**

Aspect ratio: **1:1**
Resolution: **1024×1024**
No logos. No additional text. No borders.
